Broadstairs Folk Week also has plenty of activities to keep families and children entertained, with a range of workshops, performances and events suitable for all ages.

Some of these activities include:


  • Family ceilidh dances
  • Storytelling sessions
  • Puppet shows
  • Arts and crafts workshops
  • Folk music and dance workshops
  • Street performers and buskers
  • Street parades
  • Beach games and activities


Amidst the array of events, including concerts, dances, ceilidhs, and workshops, festival-goers will have the opportunity to enjoy free live music at various pubs and bars throughout Folk Week.
